About Us

Studio 51th North is an architecture firm based in London.
The Studio covers all aspects of the design process, undertaking different ranges of projects (from hospitality to residential including commercial and retail). The scale of these projects varies greatly, from master plans to project delivery, from estates to private residences in the UK and abroad.

Our Approach

At Studio 51 North we think the right solution should be simple and clear. We like to use a contemporary language because we have to talk about our ‘era’.

‘The richness of the languages is to mix different idioms and to overlay different historical periods.’

To realise a project means to imagine a possible solution to answer a question in the best possible way’.

Studio 51th North Team

Director. Stefano Belletti

Graduated at the Politecnico di Milano in Architecture and Interior Designer, Stefano worked with GianLuigi Landoni and won the Compasso D'oro prize in 1998.In 1999 he began a collaboration with Paola Navone. Starting from 2002 he assumed the role of Head of Architecture & Interior Design in studio OTTO of Paola Navone leading project for hospitality, retail and residential properties. In 2007 he started a collaboration with Patricia Urquiola as Director for Architecture & Interior Design, following major projects for hospitality, retail and exhibition. In 2017 Stefano decides to Head Studio 51 North in London.

Project manager. Patrick Grenier

After graduating in International Logisitics in 1995, Patrick became a project manager in 1997 for The Marketing Store Worldwide. Passionate about interiors, he joined David Shave Glavey Interior Design in 2001 where he transfered his skills to high end residential projects. In 2003 he established No! Cream Interior Design, with an aim to inject diversity and colours in interiors. Projects have taken him throughout Europe and Dubai.
In 2013 he assumed the role of project and design Manager at Anna Casa Interiors. In 2017 he joined the ranks of Studio 51 North asproject manager.

Senior architect. Valentina Ravara

Graduated in Architecture in Milano, Valentina worked with various Studios including ASA Studio Albanese and Roberto Murgia. In 2008 she collaborated with PlusdesignGalley, a brand specialising in limited edition furniture and lighting. Between 2011 and 2014 she worked with Paola Navone developing projects such as Point Yamu Hotel in Thailand. The collaboration with Stefano Belletti started in 2015, delivering several projects worldwide.
